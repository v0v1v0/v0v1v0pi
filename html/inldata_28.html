Industrial Waste Ditch

Description

An industrial waste ditch located near the Naval Reactors Facility (NRF) in eastern Idaho. The NRF discharges its wastewater to this 1.65-mile-long waste ditch. The waste ditch was initially used in 1953 to dispose of non-radioactive, non-sewage industrial wastewater.

Usage

iwd

Format

A simple feature with fields:

geometry

Sequence of points connected by straight, non-self-intersecting line pieces, one-dimensional geometry.

Source

A GeoJSON file of the industrial waste ditch was created by the U.S. Geological Survey Idaho National Laboratory Project Office
