pres_elect R Documentation

Wikipedia Table of the United States of America Presidential Elections

Description

A table containing the results of the US Presidential Elections.

Usage

pres_elect

Format

A data.table data frame with 220 rows and 8 variables:

Year

Year of the election

Party

Political party

Presidential candidate

Presidential Candidate for the Political Party

Vice presidential candidate

Vice Presidential Candidate for the Political Party, if any

Popular vote

Number of popular votes

Popular vote %

% of popular votes

Electoral votes

Number of electoral votes
