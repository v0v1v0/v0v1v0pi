softdrink R Documentation

Soft Drink Data Set.

Description

Consists of 15 samples of 10 bottles where it is measured the volume of soft drink.

Format

A data frame with 15 lines and 10 columns.

x1

The first measure.

x2

The second measure.

x3

The third measure.

x4

The fourth measure.

x5

The fifth measure.

x6

The sixth measure.

x7

The seventh measure.

x8

The eigth measure.

x9

The ninth measure.

x10

The tenth measure.

Source

Montgomery, D.C.,(2001)."Introduction to Statistical Quality Control".4th ed. Wiley.

Examples


data(softdrink)
