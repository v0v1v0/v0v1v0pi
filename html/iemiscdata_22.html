exceptional_tn_waters R Documentation

Known Exceptional Tennessee Waters and Outstanding National Resource Waters

Description

A table containing the exceptional or outstanding national resource waters in Tennessee.

Usage

exceptional_tn_waters

Format

A data.table data frame with 2,994 rows and 12 variables:

HUC

HUC number

Watershed Name

Name of the watershed

Waterbody

Name of the waterbody

County

Name of the Tennessee county

Description

Description of the waterbody

Basis for Inclusion

Reason for exceptional water status

From_Lat

Begin latitude

To_Lat

End latitude

From_Long

Begin longitude

To_Long

End longitude

Inclusion Date

Date of inclusion

Revision Date

Date of revision, if any

Source

Tennessee Department of Environment and Conservation (TDEC) Division of Water Resources (DWR), Accessed 8 May 2024, The Known Exceptional Tennessee Waters and Outstanding National Resource Waters. https://archive.fo/cCgd8. Used the archive.today webpage capture for this URL for CRAN acceptance.
