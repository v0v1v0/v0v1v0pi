US EPA National Primary Drinking Water Regulations Contaminants Notes

Description

A table of notes for the primary drinking water pollutants table.

Usage

Format

A data.table data frame with 29 rows and 2 variables:

Note Number (*)

The note numbers

Notes

The notes
