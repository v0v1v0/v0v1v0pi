ow_golf_rankings R Documentation

2022 Official World Golf Rankings

Description

A dataset containing the Official World Golf Rankings in June 2022

Usage

ow_golf_rankings

Format

A data frame with 300 rows and 2 variables:

WGR_June_2022

official golf world ranking

Player_initial_last

initial of first name and complete last name of player

Source

https://www.owgr.com/archive/archive.sps?iType=1747
