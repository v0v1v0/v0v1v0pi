macaque R Documentation

Visuotactile brain areas and connections

Description

Graph model of the visuotactile brain areas and connections of the macaque monkey. The model consists of 45 areas and 463 directed connections.

Usage

macaque

Format

A directed igraph graph object with vertex attributes ‘name’ and ‘shape’.
