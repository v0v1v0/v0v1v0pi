plant_essentials R Documentation

Table of Plant Nutrients

Description

A table containing the plant macro- and micro-nutrients as chemical elements. This data set contains the content of "Table of Plant Nutrients" without the element oxidation states.

Usage

plant_essentials

Format

A data.table data frame with 11 rows and 2 variables:

Elemental Macronutrients

The plant macronutrients

Elemental Micronutrients

The plant micronutrients

Source

Irucka Ajani Embry, "Table of Plant Nutrients", Tuning to the Soil-ed Seed Vibrations For Our Collective Health, Nashville, Tennessee: Questioning the Universe Publishing, 2024, page 15. See https://www.qup.quest/soiled-seeds.html.
