example_data R Documentation

Tumour volume data over time for in-vivo studies

Description

A dataset containing the repeatedly measurements of tumour volume data over time for individual animals.

Usage

example_data

Format

A data frame with 1462 rows and 6 variables:

study

study identifier

group

group identifier

treatment

treatment type

animal_id

animal identifier

day

day after implant

tumour_volume

volume in mm3
