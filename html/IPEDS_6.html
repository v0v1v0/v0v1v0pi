complete2020 R Documentation

Completions 2020 Data

Description

This file contains the number of students receiving a degree or certificate by level of award and by race/ethnicity, gender and age categories. Data covers awards granted between July 1, 2019 and June 30, 2020. This file contains multiple records per institution. Each record will contain the total number of students receiving awards; number of men and women receiving awards: number of students receiving awards for all nine race/ethnicity categories; number of students receiving awards are also available for the following age groups: under 18, 18-24, 25-39, 40 and above.

Usage

complete2020

Format

A data frame of 15937 rows representing institutions and 19 variables

INSTITUTION_ID

Unique identification number of the institution

AWARD_LVL

Award Level code (3 = Associate's degree; 5 = Bachelor's degree; 7 = Master's degree; 9 = Doctor's degree; 10 = Postbaccalaureate or Post-master's certificate; 11 = Certificate of less than 12 weeks; 12 = Certificate of at least 12 weeks but less than 1 year; 2 = Certificate of at least 1 but less than 4 years)

TOTAL

Grand total individuals

TOTAL_M

Grand total men

TOTAL_W

Grand total women

TOTAL_NATIVE

American Indian or Alaska Native total

TOTAL_ASIAN

Asian total

TOTAL_BLACK

Black or African American total

TOTAL_HISP

Hispanic or Latino total

TOTAL_NHPI

Native Hawaiian or Other Pacific Islander total

TOTAL_WHITE

White total

TOTAL_MULT

Two or more races total

TOTAL_UNKNOWN

Race/ethnicity unknown total

TOTAL_NRA

Nonresident alien total

UND18

Ages, under 18

AGE18_24

Ages, 18-24

AGE25_39

Ages, 25-39

AGE40PLUS

Ages, 40 and above

AGE_UNKNOWN

Age unknown

Details

Provisional release data have undergone all NCES data quality control procedures. Data are imputed for nonresponding institutions.

Source

Download from Integrated Postsecondary Education Data System, https://nces.ed.gov/ipeds/datacenter/data/C2020_C.zip.
