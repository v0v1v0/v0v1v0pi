cell_salts R Documentation

27 Human Cell Salts Table

Description

A table containing the 27 inorganic substances, known as cell salts, found in Human Beings. This data set contains 2 of the 4 columns created for the "Table of 27 Cell Salts/Tissue Remedies for Human Beings".

Usage

cell_salts

Format

A data.table data frame with 27 rows and 2 variables:

Cell Salt

The cell salt English names as used in homeopathy

Molecular Formula

The molecular formula of the cell salt

Source

Irucka Ajani Embry, "Table of 27 Cell Salts/Tissue Remedies for Human Beings", Tuning to the Soil-ed Seed Vibrations For Our Collective Health, Nashville, Tennessee: Questioning the Universe Publishing, 2024, page 13. See https://www.qup.quest/soiled-seeds.html.
