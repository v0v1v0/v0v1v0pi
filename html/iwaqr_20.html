Sodium Adsorption Ratio (SAR) Calculation

Usage

SAR(df, convert_to_meq = TRUE)

Arguments

df

Dataframe containing necessary variables (Na, Ca, Mg, K).
convert_to_meq

Logical, indicating whether to convert concentrations to meq/L (default: TRUE).

Value

SAR value.
